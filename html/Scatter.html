Player search

Background

When signing a player a manager must consider the player's talent, potential, and other external factors as signing a player can cost soccer clubs millions of dollars throughout the process. Making each player research and signing efficiency critical for managers to consider in the investment process. In this player search, we plan to show player distribution against their release clauses, as well as, show their composition in terms of pace, shooting, passing, dribbling, defending and physic.

    • Findings & Conclusion

    The scatter plot can determine potential players that is needed for a specific club and minimum amount of money club needed to sign the player. Each scatter plot representing each position have different distibution of players and some players does overlap in the scatter plots showing that some players can play multiple positions. Overall, from our scatter plot we were able to create a tool to help managers to find potential players with the aligning composition to match the club's needs to improve. The plot also show that there is high amount of alternative for each position from the 3 leagues we chose. By providing basic composition metrics of each player and monetary factos like wage and release clause these visualization can be an effective tool to use for club managers.
