Soccer Synergy: Maximizing Performance with Data-Driven Decisions

Topic:

Strategic analysis of soccer club and player performance across european top leagues

Why it’s interesting:

EA FC24 is the most frequently updating and accessible data platform as it is played and assessed by soccer enthusiasts all over the world. Through this data we have the opportunity to tap into real time data and insights and capture the latest trends and development in the soccer world. Our dynamic analysis of soccer clubs and players performance can provide actionable insights that can be used by club’s management team to improve a club's overall performance through player trading and training.

Task:

  • Data abstraction (Cleaning up the data):
    • Removing non relevant data from the dataset.
    • Sort the players into different fifa versions, leagues, positions.
    • Further filtering the data set to our target leagues (Premier league, Bundesliga, and La Liga)
  • Data Analysis:
    • League analysis: Finding benchmark overall rating for each league to differentiate top players in each league
    • Club analysis: Finding each of club’s player compositions by position in terms of performance attributes to determine player trading and transfers needed.
    • Player search: Finding the suitable player with aligning performance and external factors for a club’s improvement needs
  • Data visualizations:
    • Generate data visualizations for each analysis to get in-depth findings.

Data source

The EA Sports FC 24 complete player dataset contains a wealth of information for male and female players, for example:

  • Player Basics: Name, nationality, club, league, and position.
  • Physical Attributes: Age, height, weight, and preferred foot.
  • Ratings: Overall rating, potential rating, and specific skill ratings such as pace, shooting, passing, dribbling, defending, and physicality.
  • Mental and Technical Skills: Work rates, skill moves rating, weak foot ability, and specific technical skills like finishing, crossing, ball control, and tackling.
  • Goalkeeping Attributes: For goalkeepers, skills such as diving, handling, kicking, positioning, and reflexes.
  • Status and Contract: Player value, wage, contract expiry date, and release clause if applicable.
  • International Reputation: A rating that reflects how well known and regarded the player is on the international stage.

The dataset also contains data on teams and coaches, although this data is less extensive. Data on coaches includes:

  • Name
  • DOB
  • Nationality
  • Picture of the coach

This dataset can be used for a wide range of analyses, from simple comparisons and statistical summaries to complex predictive modeling. The dataset contains 342 unique columns, with over 180,000 unique values. Overall, the data set has 19k+ players and 100+ attributes extracted from Fifa 15 - EA FC24.
